About

A family legacy, refined for a new era of craft spirits.

Heritage Archive

A family history rooted in commerce, trust, and the spirit trade.

This photograph reflects the wider family legacy from which Kingsway Spirit draws its sense of heritage, resilience, and long-term vision.

In the early 20th century, before formal banking reached many rural communities, the founding family played an important role in supporting cottage liquor manufacturing in the village of Nepti, Maharashtra.

That legacy was built not only on trade, but on relationships, local trust, and an understanding of how enterprise could sustain communities. It was a quiet yet meaningful place within the regional economy of its time.

The present founders come through the daughters’ lineage and inherited no formal share of that original business. Yet the instinct to build, connect, and create commercial pathways around craftsmanship endured across generations.

Kingsway Spirit reimagines that inheritance for a global market: developing modern supply chains for exceptional European craft liquor and introducing them to high-growth markets with care, discipline, and premium brand positioning.
